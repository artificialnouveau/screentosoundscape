Screen-to-Soundscape was supported by:

Constant

Brussels-based nonprofit focusing on art, media, and technology with critical, activist perspectives. They support experimental methodologies challenging conventional tech thinking. Constant provided funding and support enabling accessibility innovations for blind and visually impaired users.

Visit Constant

Processing Foundation

New York nonprofit promoting software literacy in visual arts and technology through open-source tools. The organization provided fellowship funding and mentorship for computer vision and sound design integration.

Visit Processing Foundation

Stimulerings Fonds Creatieve Industrie

Dutch creative industries grant organization supporting design, architecture, digital culture. Awarded Fresh Perspectives Grant funding for research, co-creator collaboration, and foundational project development.

Visit Stimuleringsfonds

SIDN fonds

Independent Dutch foundation supporting internet strengthening projects. Funds over 400 initiatives since 2015, emphasizing resilience, user empowerment, and digital societal impact.
